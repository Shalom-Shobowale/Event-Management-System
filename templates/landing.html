Features

Everything you need to run events

From creation to check-in, EventFlow handles the entire event lifecycle.

Event Creation

Create and customize events with venue details, capacity limits, seating arrangements, and VIP support.

QR Ticketing

Every guest receives a unique QR code ticket. Premium digital tickets with seat assignments and event details.

Smart Validation

Instant QR validation with duplicate prevention. Know exactly who has entered and prevent unauthorized access.

Guest Management

Register guests without accounts. Automatic seat assignment, search, filter, and export guest lists instantly.

Real-time Analytics

Track attendance rates, no-show percentages, peak check-in times, and live event metrics on your dashboard.

Mobile Scanner

Camera-based QR scanning for instant check-in. Audio feedback, real-time validation, and queue management.

Vendor Marketplace

Discover trusted vendors for your event - caterers, photographers, DJs, decorators, and more in one marketplace.

Task Management

Create tasks, set priorities, track progress, and collaborate with your team to deliver successful events.
